Lookers-on see most of the game. 旁觀者清。
Love me, love my dog. 愛屋及烏。
More haste, less speed. 欲速則不達。

No one is wise at all time. 智者千慮，必有一失。
Nothing in the world is difficult if you set your mind to it. 世上無難事，只怕有心人。
One's life is limited, but learning is endless. 生命有限，學海無涯。
Out of sight, out of mind. 眼不見，心不煩。
Pleasant hours fly past. 快樂時光去如飛。

The early bird catches the worm. 笨鳥先飛。

日常生活中常用的五星級美國口語句子

熟練地運用英語的一個重要方面就是學習並掌握英語本族者常用的生動、活潑的習語。　
1. After you.你先請。　
這是一句很常用的客套話，在進／出門,上車得場合你都可以表現一下。 (好像現在女士不願意你這麼做，特別是那些女權主義者，我還記得這麼一段話：一個女士對一個讓她先行的男士說：you do this because i am a woman?那個男士回答說：i do this not because you are a woman but because i am a man!i love this guy!　
2. I just couldn't help it.我就是忍不住。
想想看，這樣一個漂亮的句子可用於多少個場合？下面是隨意舉的一個例子：　I was deeply moved by the film and I cried and cried. I just couldn't help it.　太棒的句子了，我愛死它了。　
3. Don't take it to heart. 別往心堨h，別為此而憂慮傷神。生活實例：This test isn't that important. Don't take it to heart. 安慰人的超級句子。　
4. We'd better be off.我們該走了。It's getting late. We'd better be off .　
5. Let's face it. 面對現實吧。常表明說話人不願意逃避困難的現狀。　
　　參考例句：I know it's a difficult situation. Let's face it, OK?　很棒啊，年輕人犯錯誤，上帝都會原諒，remember?但是犯了錯誤，你必須面對他，let's face it,或者是：let's face the music.　
6. Let's get started.咱們開始幹吧。勸導別人時說：Don't just talk. Let's get started.　let's get started.　let's start.　let's do it right now.　let's hit sth.　let's rock&amp;amp;roll.　let's put our hands on sth.　
7. I'm really dead.我真要累死了。坦誠自己的感受時說：After all that work, I』m really dead.　
8. I've done my best.我已盡力了。　這句話，很有用，失敗有時難免，但是你要是可以說，i've done my best.or i spare no efforts.就不必遺憾，畢竟，man supposes,god disposes.　
9. Is that so?真是那樣嗎？常用在一個人聽了一件事後表示驚訝、懷疑。
10. Don't play games with me!別跟我耍花招！　
11. I don't know for sure.我不確切知道。　Stranger: Could you tell me how to get to the town hall?　Tom: I don't know for sure. Maybe you could ask the policeman over there.　
12. I'm not going to kid you.我不是跟你開玩笑的。　Karin: You quit the job? You are kidding.　Jack: I'm not going to kid you. I'm serious
13. That's something. 太好了，太棒了。　A: I'm granted a full scholarship for this semester.　B: Congratulations. That's something.　
14. Brilliant idea!這主意真棒！這主意真高明！　
15. Do you really mean it? 此話當真？　Michael:Whenever you are short of money, just come to me.　David: Do you really mean it?　
16. You are a great help.你幫了大忙　
17. I couldn't be more sure. 我再也肯定不過。
18. I am behind you.我支援你。　A: Whatever decision you're going to make, I am behind you.　
19. I'm broke.我身無分文。I am penniless.　
20. Mind you!請注意！聽著！(也可僅用Mind。)　模範例句：Mind you! He's a very nice fellow though bad-tempered.　
21. You can count on it.你儘管相信好了，儘管放心。　A: Do you think he will come to my birthday party?　B: You can count on it.　
22. I never liked it anyway.我一直不太喜歡這東西。
當朋友或同事不小心摔壞你的東西時就可以用上這句話給他一個臺階，打破尷尬局面：Oh, don't worry. I'm thinking of buying a new one. I never liked it anyway.　
23. That depends.看情況再說。 (on sth)　
例：I may go to the airport to meet her. But that depends.　
24. Congratulations.恭喜你，祝賀你。　
25. Thanks anyway.無論如何我還是得謝謝你。當別人盡力要幫助你卻沒幫成時，你就可以用這個短語表示謝意。　
26. It's a deal.一言為定　Harry: Haven't seen you for ages. Let's have a get-together next week.　Jenny: It's a deal　　　電影婺g常聽到，it's a deal,that is a good deal.或是兩個人打算達成某個協定或是做成某個生意會說：deal? deal!

常見英文錯誤：I meet my friends every weekend. 我每週末會和朋友見面
/******************************************************************************/
大家要注意，meet 這個動詞單獨使用，後面接人當受詞時
意思是「認識；遇到」
比如：Ted gets to meet a lot of single women at wedding parties.
(泰德在婚禮的場合得以認識許多未婚女性。)
I met some very interesting people at the conference. 
(我在研討會遇到/結識一些很有趣的人。)
但是若是要指和別人相約會面，應該要用 meet with 這個片語才對
所以「我每週末會和朋友見面」要說 I meet with my friends every weekend.
另外也可說 I get together with my friends every weekend.

假如和認識的人事先未安排好而巧遇，則是用 」>run into…. / bump into….
比如：I ran into / bumped into a high school classmate at the supermarket.
(我在超市巧遇一位高中同學。)
主動去拜訪、探視別人則應該用 visit 這個字
例如：I visited my grandma in the hospital last weekend.
(我上週末去醫院探望我的祖母。)
How often do you visit your parents / grandparents?
(你多久會探望一次你的父母/祖父母？)
如果只是表達「見到」，那就用 see 這個字即可
指的是沒有特地安排的見面，只是因為雙方同時在某個地方
像是：I see Lisa at work five days a week. 
(我一週五天都會在上班的地方見到莉莎。)
Since she moved away for her job, she』s only seen her parents every few months.
(自從她為了工作搬走後，每隔幾個月才會見到父母一次。)

商務英語今日版　(Business English Today) 六大特點
/******************************************************************************/
from : http://englishwizard.mysinablog.com/index.php?op=ViewArticle&articleId=2340162
商務英語今日版的特點就是「我短、我淺、我主動、我直接、我負責、我有時無comma」：
1) 用字寧少勿多；用字寧淺勿深—例如：三個字減到一個字；用淺字代深字，多用Thank you for + noun/gerund 代替其他字，避免無謂的賣弄！
2) 多用active voice，少用passive voice—不說Arrangements have been made for…，改說I have arranged for…。
3) 長句截短—將一句長的寫成兩句短的。
4) 平實、直接，不故弄玄虛—故弄玄虛＝奸而蠢，自以為老練奸狡，但骯髒狗屁都被人一眼看穿，掉頭掩面噴著飯笑。
5) 多用I，少用We，肯負責—怕負責任的人筆下都we、we、we過不停，認為「集體負責＝我不用負責」。
   這都不過是駝鳥心態：真要負責時，卸也卸不了。商業社會認識到避不了的殘酷現實，與其用we而被人看出誠意欠奉 (insincerity)，不如一早用I，擺明願意負責，希望取信於人，成就交易。
6) 有時省略comma—有時寫，有時合法地不寫。

依照以上原則，修改了以下十多句：
原句：Will you please send me a copy of your catalogue and price list of fax machines.
改寫：Please send me a copy of your catalogue and price list of fax machines.
說明：用字寧少勿多。

原句：We would be pleased to receive details of fax machines supplied by you, together with prices.
改寫：Please send me details of fax machines which you supply, together with prices.
說明：多用I，少用We。

原句：We feel you may be particularly interested in our Model X223 monitor, our newest model.
改寫：You may be particularly interested in our newest monitor, the X223 model.
說明：(1) 用字寧少勿多；(2) 多用I，少用We。

原句：We were very pleased to receive your letter enquiring about mobile phones and are pleased to enclose a copy of our latest illustrated catalogue.
改寫：Thank you for your letter enquiring about mobile phones.  I am pleased to enclose a copy of our latest illustrated catalogue.　
說明：(1) 用字寧少勿多；(2) 長句一分為二；(3) 多用I，少用We。

原句：We will arrange for our sales executive to call you for an appointment.
改寫：We will arrange for our sales executive to arrange to come and see you.
說明：原句的call you for an appointment也沒有問題，但arrange to come and see you更靈活，包括可用電話、電郵預約，而且，call you for an appointment有點冷，現在是come and see you，來見你呀！

原句：In the meantime, our price list is enclosed.
改寫：Meanwhile, our price list is enclosed. 或Meanwhile, we are pleased to enclose our price list.
說明：用字寧少勿多。

原句：A full range of samples has been forwarded by separate post.
改寫：A full range of samples has been sent by separate post.
說明：用字寧淺勿深。sent比forwarded更簡單、清楚。

原句：We refer to your letter of 1 July 2010.
改寫：Thank you for your letter of 1 July 2010.
說明：用字寧淺勿深，多用Thank you for＋noun。

原句：As spoken in our telecom today.
改寫：Thank you for calling me this morning.
說明：用字寧淺勿深，多用Thank you for＋gerund（gerund指用ing結尾的詞）。

原句：Please revert to me soonest possible.
改寫：I hope to hear from you soon.
說明：用字寧淺勿深。

原句：The cause of your complaint has been investigated.
改寫：I have looked into this matter.
說明：(1) 多用active，少用passive；(2) 多用I。

原句：The presentation（介紹；演示）will be conducted by May Xu.
改寫：May Xu will conduct the presentation.
說明：多用active，少用passive。

原句：Should you require any further clarification, please do not hesitate to contact the undersigned.
改寫：Please give me a call on 1234 5678 if you have any questions.
說明：(1) 用字寧淺勿深; (2) 長句截短。

原句：I should find it most helpful if you could provide samples of the various skins of which the bags are made.
改寫：It would be helpful if you could provide samples of the various skins of which the bags are made.
說明：將most helpful改為helpful，客戶向供應商這樣說most helpful，禮猊太過！

原句：Would it be convenient to arrange a visit to your showroom for next Monday 5 July at 2:30 pm?
改寫：Can we arrange a visit to our showroom for next Monday 5 July at 2:30pm?  If this is inconvenient, please call me on 1234 5678. 
說明：原句的Would it be convenient to arrange 不很合邏輯，它其實是說：Would it be convenient to you if I arrange…。改寫後分成兩句，每句都直接、簡單。

原句：I have five stationery shops in Hong Kong Island and am interested in the office stationery and office supplies you are now advertising in the Community Star (Central)（地區星報（中區））.
改寫：I have five stationery shops in Hong Kong and am interested in the office stationery and office supplies you are advertising in the Community Star (Central).
說明：用字寧少勿多；省了now。

原句：All the models illustrated can be supplied from stock at competitive prices, as shown on the price list inside the catalogue.
改寫：All the models illustrated can be supplied from stock at competitive prices as shown on the price list inside the catalogue.
說明：省略了comma。

原句：We have received a number of enquiries for mosaic marble tiles （鑲嵌大理石飾面地磚） suitable for use in luxury apartments.  It would be helpful if you could send us samples showing your ranges of floor tiles, and, if one is available, a pattern-card of the designs in which they are supplied.
改寫：We have received a number of enquiries for mosaic marble tiles suitable for use in luxury apartments.  It would be helpful if you could send us samples showing your ranges of floor tiles.  A pattern-card of the designs in which they are supplied would also be very useful.
說明：原句的if one is available其實不用寫。

原句：Further to your enquiry, I have pleasure in enclosing details of our latest DVD players.
改寫：In reply to your enquiry, I have pleasure in enclosing a leaflet showing our latest DVD players.
說明：在這裡，In reply to比further to適當，說明是回覆；enclosing a leaflet showing …較enclosing details具體（但enclosing details亦可）。

原句：May I suggest a visit to our showroom.  It would then be possible to demonstrate our fax machines and at the same time show you our wide range of office equipment.
改寫：May I suggest a visit to your showroom where you could see demonstrations of our fax machines and at the same time view our wide range of office equipment.
說明：showroom之後通常用where接，然後寫you，語氣連接。Showroom之後突然寫it would then be possible，突然「話分兩頭」，可能令對方一時跟不上。 

最後，談談查詢函和答覆函的結尾，藉此說明不需要故弄故虛。舊式寫法，會這樣：
. Your prompt reply would be appreciated. （話說得故弄玄虛，有點「屈人」，預先感謝對方早日回覆，難道想用言語套著對方早答？）
. We look forward to the pleasure of receiving an order from you soon.（理由同上，但如果對方就是不落單，說這些話也沒有用處。）
不如用平實的寫法：
. Please let me know if I can provide any further help.
. Please let me know if you have any further questions.
. Please contact me on 1234 5678 if I can provide any further help.
. I look forward to hearing from you.
. I look forward to hearing from you soon.
